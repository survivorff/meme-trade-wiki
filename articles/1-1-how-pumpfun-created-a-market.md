# How Pump.fun Created a $1B Market from Nothing

> Pump.fun didn't just launch tokens. It created an entirely new category of crypto trading — and a billion-dollar ecosystem of platforms racing to serve it.

## What You'll Learn

How a single product turned Solana into the meme coin capital of crypto, why it triggered a gold rush of trading platforms, and what the market looks like from the inside.

---

## The Surface

Most people know Pump.fun as "that site where you launch meme coins." The numbers tell a bigger story:

- $1.08B total revenue ($321M in 2024, $664M in 2025, $98M in 2026 YTD)
- 13M+ tokens created — over 1/3 of all tokens on Solana
- Peak: 71,000 tokens launched in a single day (January 2025)
- First Solana app to cross $1B in cumulative revenue
- $1.3B raised through ICO + private sale in July 2025

But Pump.fun is just the launchpad. The real story is the ecosystem it spawned.
